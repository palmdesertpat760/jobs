## 📋 About jobs

This application shows how likely different jobs in the U.S. economy are to change because of AI and automation. It uses data from the Bureau of Labor Statistics (BLS) and presents it in clear, easy-to-understand visuals. You will see which jobs face higher risks and which ones are more stable. The app uses interactive maps and charts to help you explore the data without needing any technical skills.

1. Open the application by double-clicking the "jobs" icon on your desktop or from the Start Menu.

2. The main screen will show a map or treemap of occupations. Each area shows a job or job group.

3. Hover your mouse over any area to see details like job name, risk level, and number of workers.

4. Use filters or search boxes to find specific jobs or job groups.

5. Click on sections to zoom in for more detailed information.

6. The colors on the map indicate risk levels based on AI and automation chances.

7. If you want to reset the view, find the "Reset" or "Home" button on the screen.

## 🛠 Troubleshooting

**Application does not open:**  
- Make sure your Windows system is updated.  
- Try running the installer again as Administrator (right-click > Run as Administrator).  
- Check your antivirus settings to ensure the app is not blocked.

**Data does not display or loads slowly:**  
- Confirm your internet connection is active.  
- The app needs to download the latest data when you first open it.  
- Restart the app if loading issues persist.

**Interface looks strange or buttons do not respond:**  
- Try resizing the window or maximize it.  
- Restart the app.  
- Close other programs if your computer is slow.
